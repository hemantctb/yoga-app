# AI COACHING CONTEXT (Internal)
## The Semantic Goal: The Golden Pillar
Dandasana is about finding the 90-degree axis. The vibe is immovable strength and structural clarityâ€”it is the Mountain Pose (Tadasana) of the seated world.



## The Biomechanical North Star: Vertical Spine over Neutral Pelvis
The non-negotiable is the elimination of posterior pelvic tilt. If the student is 'sitting on their tailbone' with a rounded lower back, the AI must prioritize elevating the hips on a blanket over straightening the legs.

## Transition Logic
Dandasana serves as the 'neutral' starting point for almost all seated twists and forward folds. Transitioning requires a re-establishment of the 'L' shape before moving into the next plane of motion.

## Inclusivity & Safety
For students with tight hamstrings, a micro-bend in the knees is essential to prevent the pelvis from being pulled into a tilt. For those with wrist issues, palms can face backward or the student can rest on their fists.
