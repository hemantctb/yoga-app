# AI COACHING CONTEXT (Internal)

The pose is about safety and reset. The AI should prioritize detecting signs of 'struggle' (shoulders up by ears, shallow breathing) and cue toward 'softening.'

## The Biomechanical North Star: Spinal Release
The non-negotiable is that the spine must be allowed to round and decompress. If the hips are significantly elevated from the heels due to tightness, the AI must suggest props (blanket between calves and thighs) to allow the nervous system to let go.
